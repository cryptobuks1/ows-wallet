Transact using multiple currencies
You can send, receive, and track multiple cryptocurrencies. One app is all you need.
Setup multiple wallets, each with a unique purpose
Just like bank accounts, you can setup wallets for savings, spending, budgeting, sharing, etc.
Share wallets with family, friends, or business associates
You can start a new shared wallet and invite people to join, or you can join someone else's shared wallet. Multiple people must agree to spend money.
Protect your money with safe and secure backups
There is one private key for each wallet. Export and store it in a safe place. You can use the key later to restore your wallet and spend your money.
Add your contacts
Pay friends and family using money you have in your wallet. Split dinner, send a birthday gift, or just say hello.
Get notified when you receive money
Transactions are recorded in the public blockchain. When you receive money your wallet will let you know right away.
